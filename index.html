ஹக்கீங் காக்கா மேப்ஸ்

This is intended to be a funny project and nothing else.

CONTEXT:
Our (Sri Lanka's) beloved City Planning and Water Supply minister Rauff Hakeem sometimes releases statement like "I would develop this [city name] like [a developed country name]" and after a while people stop taking it seriously. So I thought why should not I create an interactive map and rename those cities with country names. I will add more details soon (or not).
